Hi, I'm Ali

I'm of Ukrainian, Irish, and Indigenous descent and live on the lush and wild lands of coastal British Columbia.

I believe that yoga, breath-work, and meditation are profound tools to aid us in balancing the nervous system and cultivating a strong relationship between our minds, bodies, and spirits.

My students are encouraged to adapt and make the practice their own, so that they can authentically express themselves and find release. They can expect a dynamic, fluid, and grounded practice that remains tethered to the breath and anatomically based alignment.

I weave in layers of depth by leading a class that honors the historical lineage of yoga and principles of philosophy, psychology, and mysticism through precise and poignant language.

Yoga is the real deal for me - woven into every aspect of my daily life. I am humbled and inspired everyday I get to teach and share space with those who go deep into themselves to realize their limitless potential.
